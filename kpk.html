Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Tourist Spots

Peshawar City
Historical city with Qissa Khwani Bazaar and Bala Hisar Fort.

Islamia College
Peshawar – Iconic colonial-era educational building.

Bala Hisar Fort
Peshawar – Historic fort with panoramic views.

Mahabat Khan Mosque
Peshawar – Mughal-era mosque.

Khyber Pass
Historic mountain pass connecting Pakistan to Afghanistan.

Swat Valley
Known as the “Switzerland of the East” for its scenic beauty.

Malam Jabba
Swat – Ski resort and hill station.

Kalam Valley
Swat – Lush green valley surrounded by mountains.

Mingora
Swat’s main city with markets and cultural sites.

Kumrat Valley
Upper Dir – Green meadows, rivers, and forests.

Shandur Pass

Chitral Valley
Home to rich culture and beautiful landscapes.

Kalash Valley
Chitral – Unique cultural heritage of the Kalash people.

Fairy Meadows (via KPK side)
Gateway to Nanga Parbat Base Camp.

Saiful Muluk Lake
Naran – Alpine lake with a romantic legend.
