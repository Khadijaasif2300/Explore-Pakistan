Princess of Hope

A natural rock formation shaped like a princess, located in Hingol National Park.

Introduction

The **Princess of Hope** is a famous natural rock formation situated inside **Hingol National Park** in Balochistan, Pakistan. It is named so because the structure resembles a standing princess gazing into the horizon. The name was given by Hollywood actress **Angelina Jolie** during her visit to the area in 2002.

Formed naturally through centuries of wind and rain erosion, the Princess of Hope has become one of the most recognized symbols of Hingol National Park and the Makran Coastal Highway.

Key Features

  • Location: Hingol National Park, Makran Coastal Highway, Balochistan.
  • Formation: Created naturally by wind and water erosion.
  • Appearance: Resembles a princess standing tall in the desert.
  • Recognition: Named by Angelina Jolie during her UN goodwill trip.
  • Tourism: A popular stop for travelers on the coastal highway.
