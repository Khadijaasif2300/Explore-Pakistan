Moola Chotok

A hidden paradise of waterfalls and cliffs in Khuzdar, Balochistan.

Introduction

Moola Chotok is a breathtaking waterfall and gorge located in the Khuzdar District of Balochistan. Surrounded by high cliffs and rugged mountains, it is one of Pakistanâ€™s most stunning hidden tourist spots.

The waterfall flows through the narrow canyon of Moola River, creating a magical sight with crystal-clear water, lush greenery, and dramatic rocky landscapes. It is often called "Paradise in Balochistan."

Key Attractions

  • Waterfalls: Stunning cascades flowing through rocky cliffs.
  • Natural Pools: Clear water pools ideal for swimming and relaxation.
  • Adventure: Popular among trekkers, campers, and nature explorers.
  • Scenic Views: Rugged cliffs, green patches, and starry night skies.

Tourism

Moola Chotok is a dream destination for those seeking peace, adventure, and natural beauty. Though remote, it offers a unique escape into nature. The area is best explored with proper arrangements, local guidance, and camping gear.

  • Camping under the starry sky.
  • Trekking through rugged trails.
  • Swimming in natural pools.
  • Photography of waterfalls and cliffs.
