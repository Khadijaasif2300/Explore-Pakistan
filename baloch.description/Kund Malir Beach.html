Kund Malir Beach

A breathtaking desert beach along the Makran Coastal Highway in Balochistan.

Introduction

**Kund Malir Beach** is one of the most stunning beaches of Pakistan, located along the Makran Coastal Highway in Balochistan. Surrounded by golden deserts, mountains, and the deep blue Arabian Sea, it offers a unique blend of natural beauty.

Known for its crystal-clear waters and tranquil environment, Kund Malir is a favorite weekend getaway for tourists from Karachi and nearby regions. It is also part of the **Hingol National Park**, making it even more special for nature enthusiasts.

Highlights of Kund Malir

  • Location: About 250 km from Karachi, on Makran Coastal Highway.
  • Natural Beauty: Combination of desert, mountains, and sea in one landscape.
  • Hingol National Park: Close to Princess of Hope and Hinglaj Mata Temple.
  • Tourism: Popular for road trips, camping, and stargazing.

Tourism

Kund Malir is considered one of the most beautiful and cleanest beaches in Pakistan. Its peaceful surroundings, golden sand, and crystal-blue water attract thousands of tourists throughout the year.

  • Ideal for road trips and weekend getaways.
  • Perfect for camping, bonfires, and stargazing at night.
  • Close to Hingol National Park and other attractions.
  • Best visited during winters for a comfortable experience.
