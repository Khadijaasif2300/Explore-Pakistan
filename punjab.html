Punjab Tourist Spots

Badshahi Masjid
Lahore - One of the largest and most iconic mosques from the Mughal era.

Lahore Fort
Lahore - UNESCO World Heritage Site with rich Mughal architecture.

Tomb of Jahangir
Lahore - Beautiful Mughal mausoleum of Emperor Jahangir.

Rohtas Fort
Jhelum - Massive 16th-century fort built by Sher Shah Suri (UNESCO site).

Derawar Fort
Bahawalpur – Majestic desert fort in Cholistan Desert.

Noor Mahal
Bahawalpur – Italian-style palace from the Nawab era.

Hiran Minar
Sheikhupura – Mughal-era hunting resort built by Emperor Jahangir.

Katas Raj Temples
Chakwal – Ancient Hindu temple complex.

Shah Rukn-e-Alam’s Tomb
Multan – Famous Sufi shrine.

Sultan Bahu Shrine
Jhang – Revered Sufi saint’s resting place.

Murree Hills
Murree – Popular hill station with lush green hills.

Khewra Salt Mines
Jhelum – World’s second-largest salt mine, with underground chambers.

Soon Valley
Khushab – Lakes, waterfalls, and natural beauty.

Cholistan Desert
Bahawalpur – Desert jeep rallies and camel safaris.

Lahore Museum
Lahore – Historical artifacts and Gandhara sculptures.

Faisalabad Clock Tower & Bazaars
Faisalabad – Colonial-era landmark with 8 bazaars.
